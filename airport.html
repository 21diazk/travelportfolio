What NOT to Pack in a Carry-on

When flying on an airplane, airport security and luggage checking can easily add extra stress and time onto the trip. If security finds something that is not permitted on the plane, it will most likely be thrown in the garbage, which can be a big waste of money.These items are what you should either leave at home and repurchase at the destination, or find another way to keep them:

  • Liquids over 3.4oz. All liquids must fit into one quart sized ziplock bag
  • Sharp Objects. Small nail scissors and safety razors are typically allowed
  • Insect repellent, cleaning products, etc.
  • Alcohol. If necessary to pack, pack in checked luggage
  • Mace/pepper spray, self defence items.
